Plant Diseases

Apple - Apple Scab

Cause: Fungal infection caused by Venturia inaequalis.

Treatment: Apply fungicides like captan or mancozeb during early growth stages.

Apple - Black Rot

Cause: Caused by Botryosphaeria obtusa. It affects leaves, fruit, and bark.

Treatment: Prune infected branches and use fungicides like thiophanate-methyl.

Apple - Cedar Apple Rust

Cause: Caused by Gymnosporangium juniperi-virginianae. Requires both apple and cedar trees to complete its lifecycle.

Treatment: Remove nearby cedar trees. Apply fungicides such as myclobutanil during early leaf development.

Apple - Healthy

Cause: N/A (healthy condition).

Treatment: Regular inspection, good cultural practices, and preventative fungicides.

Blueberry - Healthy

Cause: N/A (healthy condition).

Treatment: Adequate irrigation, mulching, and fertilization ensure plant health.

Cherry - Powdery Mildew

Cause: Caused by Podosphaera clandestina fungus. Thrives in warm, dry conditions.

Treatment: Use sulfur-based fungicides and ensure good air circulation by pruning.

Cherry - Healthy

Cause: N/A (healthy condition).

Treatment: Prevent disease by monitoring and maintaining orchard hygiene.

Corn (Maize) - Cercospora Leaf Spot Gray Leaf Spot

Cause: Caused by Cercospora zeae-maydis. Wet, warm climates promote its growth.

Treatment: Use resistant hybrids, rotate crops, and apply fungicides like strobilurins.

Corn (Maize) - Common Rust

Cause: Caused by Puccinia sorghi fungus. It forms orange-red pustules on leaves.

Treatment: Plant resistant hybrids and apply fungicides such as azoxystrobin or propiconazole.

Corn (Maize) - Northern Leaf Blight

Cause: Caused by Exserohilum turcicum fungus. Cool, moist environments favor its growth.

Treatment: Use resistant varieties and fungicides. Rotate crops and destroy crop debris.

Corn (Maize) - Healthy

Cause: N/A (healthy condition).

Treatment: Maintain good practices and monitor for pests/diseases.

Grape - Black Rot

Cause: Caused by Guignardia bidwellii. High humidity accelerates the infection.

Treatment: Prune infected parts and use fungicides like mancozeb or myclobutanil.

Grape - Esca (Black Measles)

Cause: A fungal complex of Phaeoacremonium and Phaeomoniella species.

Treatment: Remove infected vines. Avoid injuries to trunks and branches.

Grape - Leaf Blight (Isariopsis Leaf Spot)

Cause: Caused by Pseudocercospora vitis fungus.

Treatment: Use fungicides like chlorothalonil. Maintain good canopy airflow.

Grape - Healthy

Cause: N/A (healthy condition).

Treatment: Regular vine maintenance and preventative measures.

Orange - Haunglongbing (Citrus Greening)

Cause: Bacterial disease caused by Candidatus Liberibacter. Transmitted by psyllids.

Treatment: Remove infected trees. Control psyllid populations with insecticides.

Peach - Bacterial Spot

Cause: Caused by Xanthomonas campestris pv. pruni. Spread by wind and rain.

Treatment: Use copper-based bactericides. Prune to improve airflow.

Peach - Healthy

Cause: N/A (healthy condition).

Treatment: Monitor and maintain good orchard practices.

Pepper (Bell) - Bacterial Spot

Cause: Caused by Xanthomonas bacteria. Spread through water splashes.

Treatment: Use resistant varieties and copper fungicides. Avoid overhead irrigation.

Pepper (Bell) - Healthy

Cause: N/A (healthy condition).

Treatment: Proper fertilization and pest monitoring.

Potato - Early Blight

Cause: Caused by Alternaria solani fungus. Prominent in warm, wet conditions.

Treatment: Apply fungicides like chlorothalonil. Use certified seed potatoes.

Potato - Late Blight

Cause: Caused by Phytophthora infestans. Common in cool, wet climates.

Treatment: Use fungicides (e.g., mancozeb) and destroy infected plants immediately.

Potato - Healthy

Cause: N/A (healthy condition).

Treatment: Regular monitoring and crop care.

Raspberry - Healthy

Cause: N/A (healthy condition).

Treatment: Regular pruning, mulching, and pest control.

Soybean - Healthy

Cause: N/A (healthy condition).

Treatment: Crop rotation and balanced fertilization.

Squash - Powdery Mildew

Cause: Caused by Podosphaera xanthii or Erysiphe cichoracearum fungi.

Treatment: Apply sulfur fungicides and remove heavily infected leaves.

Strawberry - Leaf Scorch

Cause: Caused by Diplocarpon earliana fungus.

Treatment: Remove infected leaves and use fungicides like captan.

Strawberry - Healthy

Cause: N/A (healthy condition).

Treatment: Proper irrigation and pest management.

Tomato - Bacterial Spot

Cause: Caused by Xanthomonas campestris pv. vesicatoria.

Treatment: Use resistant varieties and copper fungicides.

Tomato - Early Blight

Cause: Caused by Alternaria solani.

Treatment: Rotate crops and apply fungicides like mancozeb.

Tomato - Late Blight

Cause: Caused by Phytophthora infestans.

Treatment: Use fungicides and remove infected plants.

Tomato - Leaf Mold

Cause: Caused by Passalora fulva.

Treatment: Increase ventilation and use fungicides.

Tomato - Septoria Leaf Spot

Cause: Caused by Septoria lycopersici.

Treatment: Remove infected leaves and apply fungicides.

Tomato - Spider Mites (Two-Spotted Spider Mite)

Cause: Caused by spider mites (Tetranychus urticae).

Treatment: Use miticides or insecticidal soap.

Tomato - Target Spot

Cause: Caused by Corynespora cassiicola.

Treatment: Apply fungicides and prune infected foliage.

Tomato - Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl Virus

Cause: Transmitted by whiteflies.

Treatment: Use reflective mulches and insecticides.

Tomato - Tomato Mosaic Virus

Cause: Spread by contact with infected plants or tools.

Treatment: Sanitize tools and remove infected plants.

Tomato - Healthy

Cause: N/A (healthy condition).

Treatment: Monitor regularly and maintain good cultural practices.
